Who We Are

Pokémon Go Mount Airy, NC is a friendly group of Pokémon Go players coming together to help each other with raids, PvP or anything related to the game! All levels of players are welcome from new players to seasoned veterans! We are based in the town of Mount Airy located in Surry County in North Carolina.

How to Join

New to our community or visiting? Follow these steps to get started and join us:

  1. Like our Facebook Page (Pokémon Go - Mount Airy) to stay up to date on news and announcements for the group
  2. Join our Facebook Group (Pokémon Go - Mount Airy - Surry County Raids) to share your trainer code and meet other players
  3. After joining our Facebook Group, post a comment on this post to be added to our Facebook Messenger group chats. We have a main chat for raiding, a PvP chat for our monthly tournaments and a Niantic Wayfarer chat for submitting new stops and gyms

Raid Hour

Every Wednesday night we have a group led raid hour. Like the Facebook Page for announcements and schedules for raid hours.

Silph Arena PvP Tournaments

Every month we host a monthly Silph Arena PvP Tournament with a gift card prize. In order to participate, ask to join our PvP group chat.
